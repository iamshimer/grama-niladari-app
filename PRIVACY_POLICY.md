# Privacy Policy for Grama Niladari App

**Last Updated:** January 18, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Grama Niladari Application ("the App") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information. This App is designed for government officials (Grama Niladhari officers) to manage household and citizen data.

By using this App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Household Information:
- House number, address, contact information
- Property details, utilities, vehicles
- Location coordinates (GPS)
- House and family photographs

### Individual Member Information:
- Full name, NIC number, birth certificate
- Date of birth, gender, marital status
- Contact information, photographs
- Parent information, education, employment
- Government support details, special conditions
- Business and property information

### Technical Information:
- Device information, app usage data
- Error logs, authentication credentials

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- Administrative management of households and individuals
- Government services delivery
- Statistical analysis and reporting
- Welfare distribution
- Emergency response
- Census and surveys

---

## 3. Data Security and Encryption

### Encryption:
- **AES-256 encryption** with unique initialization vectors
- All sensitive data encrypted before storage
- Secure key management on backend servers
- HTTPS/TLS for data transmission

### Access Control:
- Role-based access (Grama Niladhari officers and administrators only)
- Audit logs for all data access
- Automatic session timeout

### Storage:
- Firebase Cloud Firestore (encrypted at rest)
- Secure Firebase Storage for media files

---

## 4. Data Sharing

**We share data with:**
- Authorized government departments
- Firebase/Google (infrastructure provider)
- Google Maps (location services)

**We do NOT:**
- Sell personal data
- Use data for marketing
- Share with unauthorized parties

---

## 5. User Rights

Citizens can:
- Request access to their data
- Request corrections
- Request information about data usage

Contact your local Grama Niladhari office to exercise these rights.

---

## 6. Data Retention

Data is retained as long as:
- Household remains in the division
- Required for administrative purposes
- Mandated by regulations

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

We collect children's information as part of household records for:
- Accurate census data
- Education and welfare services
- Child welfare monitoring

Parents can request to review their children's information.
