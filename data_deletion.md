# Data Deletion Policy

## How to Request Data Deletion

If you wish to have your personal data deleted from the Grama Niladari App, please follow these steps:

### For Citizens:

1. **Contact Your Local Office:**
   - Visit your local Grama Niladhari office
   - Submit a written request for data deletion
   - Provide proof of identity

2. **Email Request:**
   - Send an email to: [shimerahamad4@gmail.com]
   - Include: Full name, NIC number, house number
   - Attach proof of identity

3. **Processing Time:**
   - Requests processed within 30 days
   - You will receive confirmation via email

### What Gets Deleted:

- Your personal information
- Family member records (if head of household)
- Photos and documents
- All associated data

### What Cannot Be Deleted:

Some data may be retained if:
- Required by law
- Part of ongoing government processes
- Needed for legal obligations
